Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we ("we," "us," or "our") collect, use, and protect information when you use the Tasks application and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to operate the Service:

  • Account information — when you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, profile picture, and a unique Google account identifier.
  • Authentication data — session tokens and cryptographic keys used to keep you signed in.
  • Usage data — basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamps, used for security and debugging.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Your Data in the Application

Tasks you create in the application are encrypted on your device before being stored. We do not read, sell, or share the contents of your tasks.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers that help us operate the Service (such as hosting and authentication providers), and only as needed to provide the Service, or as required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the most recent changes were made.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at brayden@projectbabbage.com.
